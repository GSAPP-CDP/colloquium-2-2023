Design Proposal

This is a proposal about redefining architectural and urban systems to achieve low carbon emissions.

Currently, most of the land in US is Suburban Context. However, compared to people living in a carless urban context, the residents living in the suburban context produce 4 times more carbon footprint every year. One of reasons is because the more centralized city form allows people to consume less energy consumption on daily transportation. There are three scales/ideas/directions for this project



There are three scales/ideas/directions for this project

1. Centralize the city to only use Half Land Occupation (urban)

2. Vertical block which will create a new urban community and social context (building

3. Robotic housing unit will bring in the idea of Half Size Home (units/floor plan/ interior)
